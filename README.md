# Stars & Stripes Canvas 🎆
**A 4th of July Interactive Storybook**

This is a three-page animated storybook about a town's Fourth of July night — told through HTML and CSS only. 

---

## What it is

You click through three chapters, each one showing a different moment of the night:

1. **Everyone heads to the shore** — The whole town walks to the beach as the sky turns dark blue. You can see silhouette families marching across the screen — a couple carrying a blanket, a kid running ahead, a person walking their dog, someone waving a little flag.

2. **Sparklers & ice cream** — A boardwalk at night. An ice cream cart rolls across with a spinning bell and striped umbrella. Kids hold up glowing cones. Three people wave sparklers with golden sparks flying off the tips.

3. **The sky catches fire** — A crowd of silhouettes stands on the beach, all heads tilted back looking up. Fireworks burst overhead in red, white, blue, and gold. Their colors reflect on the ocean below.


---

## How it works

- **All animations are CSS** — the walking people, rolling cart, fireworks, waves, stars, and sparks are pure `@keyframes`
- **Audio** — each section has an `<audio>` tag with ocean waves for pages 1 & 2, and fireworks sound for page 3. Browsers require user interaction before playing sound, so a visible player control is included
